  • Job creation & economic impact
  • Greater access to innovative medicines
  • Alignment with Egypt Vision 2030
  • Strengthened local supply chain

05 — Press Release

Official Announcement Draft

For Immediate Release

Novartis Egypt Expands Manufacturing Facility,
Reinforcing Commitment to Egyptian Healthcare

Novartis Egypt announced the expansion of its local manufacturing facility, marking a significant step in strengthening its presence in the Egyptian healthcare sector. This expansion reflects the company's long-term commitment to supporting Egypt's vision for improving healthcare access and advancing local production capabilities.

The expanded facility will enable Novartis to broaden its manufacturing portfolio, ensuring the availability of high-quality and innovative treatments for patients across the country. This move aligns with national efforts to enhance healthcare infrastructure and reduce dependency on imported medicines.

Commenting on the expansion, a Novartis Egypt spokesperson said: "We are proud to deepen our investment in Egypt and contribute to the development of the local healthcare ecosystem. This expansion allows us to better serve patients and support the country's healthcare priorities."

Egypt's Minister of Health, Dr. Khaled Abdel Ghaffar also highlighted the importance of such investments, stating: "This expansion represents a valuable step towards strengthening Egypt's pharmaceutical manufacturing capabilities and ensuring sustainable access to essential medicines for our citizens."

In addition to improving healthcare access, the expansion is expected to create new job opportunities and contribute to the country's economic growth.

Novartis Egypt remains committed to innovation, collaboration, and delivering impactful healthcare solutions that improve patients' lives.
